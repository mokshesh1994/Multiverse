We're going underwater this week! Jace and Kyra grew up together in the underwater city of Cirrus. But when Jace starts to ask questions that his teachers won't answer, he decides that it's up to the two of them to uncover the truth. Join Jace and Kyra as they search to learn more about the history behind The Sunken City of Cirrus!
